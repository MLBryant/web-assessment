About my kingdom:

    I live in a sleepy little community of townhomes and duplexes. The usual quiet only occasionally broken by the stacatto of gunfire or the song of a truck peeling out. Being neighborly folk, we greet each other with a howdy-do or a tip of the hat when passing by. Even the dogs say hello or leave messages for each other in their own special ways. So come on down to get away from the big-city feeling of living in the state capital.
